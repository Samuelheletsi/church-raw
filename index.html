About Us

Royalties Youth Church (RYC) is committed to raising leaders and transforming lives through spiritual growth, fellowship, and community service. Our programs focus on youth empowerment, faith development, and community outreach.

We aim to create a vibrant and nurturing environment where young people can thrive, discover their potential, and contribute positively to society.
